Supabase is like a super-smart filing cabinet that remembers everything about your app. It's a "Backend as a Service" - basically a ready-made database with superpowers.

## How Supabase Works in PayPost:

### 1. **Data Storage Tables**
Supabase organizes information in tables (like Excel spreadsheets):

**Users Table:**
```

|----|-------|------|------------|----------------|
| 1  | john@email.com | participant | 2024-12-29 | 45.50 |
| 2  | sarah@email.com | creator | 2024-12-29 | 0.00 |
```

**Surveys Table:**
```
| id | title | creator_id | reward_amount | status |
|----|-------|------------|---------------|--------|
| 1  | "Rate My App" | 2 | 5.00 | active |
| 2  | "Food Preferences" | 2 | 3.00 | completed |
```

**Responses Table:**
```
| id | survey_id | user_id | answers | completed_at |
|----|-----------|---------|---------|--------------|
| 1  | 1 | 1 | ["Great app!", "5 stars"] | 2024-12-29 |
```

### 2. **Real-Time Updates**
Supabase can instantly notify your app when data changes:
```javascript

  .channel('survey-responses')
```

### 3. **API Endpoints (How Apps Talk to Database)**
Supabase automatically creates ways for your app to:
- **GET** data: "Show me all available surveys"
- **POST** data: "Save this new survey"
- **UPDATE** data: "Mark this survey as completed"
- **DELETE** data: "Remove this old survey"

## What is Movement Blockchain?
Movement is like a digital bank that never closes, never loses money, and never lies about transactions. It's a blockchain (a chain of transaction records) that handles cryptocurrency payments.

## How Blockchain Works (Simple Explanation):

### 1. **The Ledger Book**
Imagine a notebook that:
- Records every transaction ever made
- Can never be erased or changed
- Is copied to thousands of computers worldwide
- Everyone can read it, but no one can cheat

```
Block #1: Alice sent 5 MOVE to Bob
Block #2: Bob sent 2 MOVE to Charlie  
Block #3: Charlie sent 1 MOVE to Alice
[This continues forever, creating a "chain" of blocks]
```

### 2. **MOVE Tokens (The Currency)**
MOVE tokens are like digital dollars that:
- Live on the Movement blockchain
- Can be sent instantly to anyone
- Cost almost nothing to transfer
- Are secured by cryptography (impossible to counterfeit)

### 3. **How PayPost Uses Movement Blockchain:**

**When You Create a Survey:**

```move
    let total_cost = reward_per_response * max_responses;
    transfer_coins(creator, contract_address, total_cost);
}

// Rule 2: Participants get paid automatically when they complete surveys
public fun complete_survey(participant: &signer, survey_id: u64, answers: vector<String>) {
    // Verify survey exists and has funds
    // Save participant's answers
    // Send reward to participant automatically
    transfer_coins(contract_address, participant_address, reward_amount);
}
```

### 2. **Contract Address (Where It Lives):**

### 3. **What the Smart Contract Does Automatically:**

**Survey Creation Process:**
1. Creator wants to create survey for 10 people, paying 5 MOVE each
