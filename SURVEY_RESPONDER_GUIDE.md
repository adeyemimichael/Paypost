## ðŸŽ¯ Complete User Flow for Survey Participants

### Step 1: Visit PayPost Homepage
- Go to `http://localhost:5174`
- See the beautiful landing page with "Turn Your Opinions Into Earnings"

### Step 2: Choose Your Role
- Click **"Choose Role & Start"** button
- **Role Selection Modal** appears with two options:
  - **Survey Participant** (for earning rewards)
  - **Survey Creator** (for creating surveys)
- Select **"Survey Participant"**
- Click **"Continue as Participant"**

### Step 3: Connect Wallet via Privy
- Privy modal opens with login options:
  - Email
  - Google
  - Twitter
- Choose your preferred method
- Privy creates an embedded wallet automatically (no seed phrases!)
- Wallet connects and you're logged in

### Step 4: Navigate to Earn Rewards
- You're now logged in with "Participant" badge in navbar
- Click **"Earn Rewards"** in the navigation menu
- This takes you to the Feed page

### Step 5: Browse Available Surveys
- See the **"Earn MOVE Tokens"** page
- **Earnings Dashboard** shows your current stats
- **Content Filter** with helpful tip: "Click 'Earn Rewards' to see available surveys"
- Click **"Earn Rewards"** filter button (highlighted in blue with green dot)

### Step 6: View Available Surveys
You'll see 4 active surveys:

1. **Web3 Gaming Survey** - Earn 0.5 MOVE (5 min)
2. **DeFi User Experience Study** - Earn 0.3 MOVE (3 min)  
3. **Community Poll: Favorite Blockchain** - Earn 0.1 MOVE (1 min)
4. **Product Feedback: Mobile Wallet UX** - Earn 0.4 MOVE (4 min)

### Step 7: Complete a Survey
- Click **"Start Survey"** on any survey card
- **Survey Modal** opens with:
  - Survey info (reward amount, time estimate, responses)
  - Progress bar
  - Questions (multiple choice, rating, text, checkbox)
- Answer each question
- Click **"Next"** to proceed
- Click **"Submit Survey"** on final question

### Step 8: Earn Rewards
- **Success animation** appears
- See **"Survey Completed! Earned X MOVE"** message
- Rewards are sent to your wallet instantly
- **Earnings Dashboard** updates with new total

### Step 9: Continue Earning
- Return to feed to complete more surveys
- Track your progress in the earnings dashboard
- Tip creators if you enjoyed their surveys
